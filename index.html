Questions, answered

Does Otto post automatically for me?

Otto does all the heavy lifting — it pulls the vehicle, writes the listing, picks the photos and preps everything for each channel. For Facebook Marketplace and similar platforms, you give a final one-tap approval to publish. This keeps your accounts safe and fully within each platform's rules, while still saving you hours every day.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

No. If you can use Facebook on your phone, you can use Otto. Setup is guided, and you'll have a real person (the founder) to help you get going.

Will this get my Facebook account banned?

No. Otto never logs in and posts as a robot. You stay in control of publishing, which is exactly how the platforms want it to work.

Is my lead really protected as mine?

Yes. Every lead is stamped with its source, a timestamp, the vehicle, and your name — giving you documented proof the opportunity came from your efforts.

What does the $25 deposit do?

It reserves your Founding Member spot and locks in discounted lifetime pricing. It's fully refundable — if Otto isn't right for you, you get it back, no questions asked. Prefer not to pay yet? Use the form to join the waitlist instead.
